History of Italy

- Italy just off the back of World War II

- Economic boom from 1950s-1970s

- Lots of international trade, trade with manufactured goods

- Lots of people immigrating to other places in Italy

- A lot of southern migrants looking for work in the northern labor market

- Many families coming to live in northern cities, cities unprepared for it

- Changes in class structure during economic miracle

- TVs became popular, families living together became smaller (ex. grandparents, aunts, uncles no longer at home)

- Less women in the workforce at this time

- Less people attending church, important because religion is a big part of Italy

- More people with more money, lots of people going to the cinema
