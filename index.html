HISTORY OF ITALY HORROR GENRE FILMS

Italian Horror Films of the 1960s-70s

Introduction

Italian cinema has a long and diverse history, but this website was made to explore one genre of cinema: Horror! No matter the time or place, the genre of horror has always pushed the bounds of what is acceptable to show on the silver screen. Follow me (if you dare) as I explore how the Italian horror films of the 1960s and 1970s, and how they challenged societal expectations of the time!
